Enactus Day 1.0

March 24-25, 2018

Enactus Day 1.0 was a huge success and attracted a lot of students to explore this field and society.
On the first day, first edition of our flagship event, Social Budgeting Convention. 60 students were divided into 30 teams. Each team was given a real existing village along with some basic statistics and problems going on in the village. Colonel Gurmeet Sethi witnessed the event as the chief guest. The basic idea was to widen the horizons and change the perspective on the entrepreneurship landscape in India. An inhouse competition to bring out the best of something Enactus is truly adept at: social entrepreneurship.

Social entrepreneurs identify resources where people only see problems. They view the villagers as the solution, not the passive beneficiary. They begin with the assumption of competence and unleash resources in the communities theyâ€™re serving..

The Champions League Auction, was the second day event of Enactus Day 1.0. Participants took active interest and their energy & vibes helped make the event a success. The event was in two rounds: Auction and Playing FIFA 2018 with the team formed. The League tournament was very exciting and more competitive, with best of the gamers playing it was a close shot. The event was a success with every going peacefully and with loads of fun.
